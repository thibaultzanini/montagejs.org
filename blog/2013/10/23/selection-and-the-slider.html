Selection, and a streamlined Slider

October 23 2013 Posted by Stuart Knightley

There were just a couple of merged changes this week, and both were tidying up.

The RangeController had an improvement to the selection which reduces the complexity of the code and adds some new features, such as ensuring that that all items in selection are members of the content. Matte and Native were updated for compatibility.

And in Digit Slider was streamlined to use fewer elements, simplifying the HTML and CSS.
