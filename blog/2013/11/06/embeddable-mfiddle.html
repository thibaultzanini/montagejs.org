Embeddable MFiddle

November 06 2013 Posted by Stuart Knightley

Montage saw a small fix for the translate-composer last week. And MFiddle gained a great new power.

The power to be embedded!

By going to /embed and /preview you can get the source and result of a given MFiddle respectively. For the above fiddle the two URLs are http://montagejs.github.io/mfiddle/embed/#!/7328022 and http://montagejs.github.io/mfiddle/preview/#!/7328022

Look out for interactive examples appearing in the documentation soon.
