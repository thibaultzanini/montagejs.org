New getting started guides and more

December 04 2013 Posted by Stuart Knightley

The quest to make our documentation the best it can be continues, with the addition of Composer JSDocs, a big normalization of JSDocs and exciting new set-up and Hello MontageJS tutorials. They are live at Getting Started with MontageJS and Hello MontageJS.

Montage recieved a fix to call beforeChangeListeners before the change happens and the Digit Select was fixed to prevent an undefined item in content breaking drawing.

Some bugs were found in Mr v0.14.0 and so Mr v0.14.2 was released to fix them. Mop v0.13.5 was released to pick up these changes and also included a fix for independent modules creating different bundles.

Minit v0.4.1 was also released to bump all the dependency versions.

Releases

Mr v0.14.2, Mop v0.13.5 and Minit v0.4.1
